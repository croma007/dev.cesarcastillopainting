About

Let us help you bring your home to life with the brilliance of quality paint finishes inside and out.

Whether it’s the first brush stroke on new construction or reviving an old classic, Cesar Castillo Painting has the experience and craftsmen to apply the best paints available and create the home of your dreams.

Founded in 2000, Cesar Castillo Painting provides both interior and exterior painting contractor services to homeowners and businesses in the Greater Boston Area. (We are fully licensed and insured). We also offer power-washing services to prepare for exterior painting applications or to simply clean your home or deck.

As a member of the Professional Painters & Decorators of America, we are committed to achieving total customer satisfaction, employing only professional painters and using both the latest technologies and proven techniques. See our Company Mission Statement.

    Mission Statement

    • Our Company is devoted to the life-cycle costing* belief that by providing the absolute best products and techniques the paint job will be more durable, look better longer, and essentially cost less in the long term.

    • Our company takes pride in utilizing paints and materials that will be less harmful to the environment Our company is devoted to projects as long term investments, keeping us from becoming volume sales oriented.
    • We examine your projects thoroughly to employ the optimal techniques.
    • Our company does not employ summer help. All of our painters are year-round employees.
    • We provide minimal number of painters (a maximum of five) to maintain continuity and span of control.
    • Our painters are friendly and courteous and are provided with training on a daily basis with emphasis on safety and painting as a craft.
    • Our company is proud to be a member of “Professional Painters & Decorators of America”, an organization that provides us with the latest product changes, innovative tools, and management strategies.
    • Our customer satisfaction is exceptional. We can help you to find the way to resolve related issues with your home, thus the “CAN DO” motto was inspired.
    • Our customers believe in our commitments to their projects.

    Exterior Painting

    At Cesar Castillo Painting, they know that paint beautifies your home’s exterior and also protects it from the environment. This critical job must be done to perfection. They use only small crews of professional, year-round painters. Contact them for jobs big and small.

    Interior Painting

    Inside painting provides character to your home and can add tremendous value when selling your property. As our primary service, the crews at Cesar Castillo Painting have years of experience in interior painting. Their skill and craftsmanship produce eye-catching detail. Additionally, they utilize environmentally-friendly materials and commercial-grade plastics in lieu of dropcloths, thus reducing allergens.

    Power Washing

    Washing your home is a low-cost way to improve the look of wood, stucco, decking, walkways, and vinyl siding. It is also recommended by paint manufacturers between paint jobs.

    Power Washing will remove:

    • Dirt
    • Mildew (mildew spores)
    • Environmental Pollution
    • Pollen Dust

    Power Washing is a great idea before:

    • Selling Your Home
    • Get-togethers & Parties
    • Backyard Functions
    • Graduations/Weddings
